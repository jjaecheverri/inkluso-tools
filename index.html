IN-KluSo / Research Layer

Your idea.
Our output.

Two tools that let you submit a structured intelligence request and receive a published, branded IN-KluSo report — researched, verified, and committed to this repository.

How it works

This is a human-in-the-loop system. You define the request. We run the research. The output lands back here — verified, sourced, and formatted to IN-KluSo editorial standards.

Step 01
Fill the form

Choose your tool — CI or OPS — and enter your parameters.

Step 02
Generate request

Download or copy your structured JSON packet.

Step 03
Paste into Tasklet

Hand off the packet to the Tasklet execution engine.

Step 04
Output published

Your branded report appears in /outputs/ and goes live.
